Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Make autonomous outdoor maintenance accessible to every commercial site that needs it.

Our Vision

03

The Problem We're Solving

Outdoor maintenance labor is structurally broken. We're not patching it, we're replacing it.

Structural Labor Shortage

Commercial outdoor maintenance is chronically understaffed. Facilities managers face growing gaps in snow removal, mowing, and grounds crews, with no structural fix in sight.

Liability & Compliance Risk

Uncleared walkways, undocumented service, and reactive-only maintenance create legal and safety exposure for property owners, municipalities, and operators.

Escalating OpEx

Labor, insurance, equipment, and fuel costs compound every year. Facilities budgets are being stretched, with no improvement in output or accountability.
