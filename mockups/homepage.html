Is Your Site a Fit?

Strong deployments start with a defined route, recurring task, and a team ready to support assessment.

✓

Strong Fit

  • Commercial or institutional site
  • Defined recurring routes or zones
  • Labor, safety, schedule, or documentation pressure
  • Snow, mowing, sweeping, brush, or multi-season use case
  • Map, aerial image, or route layout available
  • Staging and charging plan possible
  • Internal owner for facilities, safety, or procurement
✕

Not Likely a Fit

  • Residential property
  • One-time service
  • No defined route or zone
  • No staging or charging area
  • Expecting guaranteed ROI before pilot
  • Expecting fully hands-off operation in all conditions
