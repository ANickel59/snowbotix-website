Leadership

Built by robotics and outdoor equipment operators.

Outdoor autonomy has to work outside the lab. It has to run in cold, heat, rain, snow, dust, uneven terrain, tight spaces, and active commercial sites. It has to be safe enough for customers to trust, rugged enough for field work, and practical enough to support.

RoboWorkX is building Snowbotix around that standard: robotics, outdoor equipment, software, service, and deployment experience working together.

Founder & Leadership

Dr. Sasi Prabhakaran

Founder & CEO, RoboWorkX Inc.

Roboticist and founder focused on bringing autonomous systems into real outdoor work. His technical background spans autonomous navigation, dynamics, estimation, and control across unmanned systems, including underactuated vehicles, UAVs, spacecraft simulators, and robotic platforms operating in unknown or GPS-denied environments.

Before founding Snowbotix, he held research roles at Syracuse University, New Mexico State University, the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, and the Indian Institute of Science.

Sasi founded RoboWorkX to apply that technical foundation to a practical problem: outdoor maintenance work that is hazardous, repetitive, hard to staff, and difficult to perform consistently in weather. His current focus is AIB, Autonomy-in-a-Box.

Klaus Hahn

COO, RoboWorkX Inc. / Snowbotix

Brings more than 30 years of leadership experience across outdoor power equipment, commercial cleaning equipment, product development, marketing, supply chain, and international operations.

His background includes senior executive roles at Greenworks Tools, Techtronic Industries, and Kärcher, with responsibility across North America, Europe, product strategy, business development, customer operations, logistics, and commercialization. He has worked with major outdoor and power-tool categories tied to brands and platforms customers already understand, including Greenworks, Ryobi, Milwaukee, and Kärcher.

At RoboWorkX, Klaus helps translate robotics into a commercially deployable equipment business. His role strengthens the bridge between Sasi's autonomy technology and the practical requirements of outdoor equipment buyers: product configuration, serviceability, customer proof, channel readiness, cost control, supply chain, and scale.
