How We Work

What we look for.

We are solving a real physical-world problem. The candidates who fit best here understand that building in the field is different from building in a controlled environment, and that useful technology has to be reliable before it is impressive.

Field First

Real-environment thinking

We operate in snow, mud, ice, and variable conditions. Candidates who have worked in physical environments, robotics, grounds, construction, field service, or manufacturing, understand constraints that lab and office environments do not surface.

Focused

Do one thing well before adding the next

We do not need generalists. We need people who are extremely good at one thing and can operate without broad supervision.

Commercial

Useful is more important than impressive

The product has to work for commercial customers with real operations and real problems. Engineering and commercial decisions should be weighted by what makes the product more reliable and more useful, not more technically ambitious.
