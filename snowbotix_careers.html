What we are building

Snowbotix is focused on autonomous outdoor maintenance for real-world field conditions, including snow, mowing, sweeping, and related commercial site operations.

Our work sits at the intersection of robotics, field reliability, commercial operations, and customer deployment. The product has to work outside, in variable conditions, for organizations that depend on consistent site operations.

What tends to matter here

We value people who are comfortable with early-stage ambiguity, field constraints, and practical execution.

Good fit is more likely if you:

  • Have built, operated, sold, deployed, or supported products in real-world environments
  • Can work with limited structure
  • Care more about reliability and usefulness than novelty
  • Understand that field robotics is an operating problem, not only a technical problem
  • Are comfortable with a small team where priorities may change as customer needs become clearer
