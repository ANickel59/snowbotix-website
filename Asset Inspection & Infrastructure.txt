Asset Inspection
Asset Inspection

Section headline
Automated asset inspection with sub-inch mapping accuracy.

Body copy
Snowbotix turns routine outdoor maintenance routes into high-resolution inspection passes. As the robot clears, sweeps, mows, patrols, or travels mapped routes, it uses LiDAR, computer vision, RTK-GNSS, HD cameras, thermal sensing, and onboard positioning to capture detailed infrastructure conditions across sidewalks, paths, curbs, crossings, ramps, plazas, lots, rights-of-way, and controlled outdoor zones.

For sites that need more than visual spot checks, Snowbotix can generate sub-inch accurate infrastructure maps and geocoded inspection records that help teams understand what changed, where it changed, and what requires follow-up.

What Snowbotix can inspect

Pavement cracks, potholes, settlements, heaves, and drainage issues.
Sidewalks, curb ramps, crosswalks, landings, and accessible routes.
Automated ADA compliance checks for trip hazards, slope issues, detectable warnings, and route obstructions.
Curbs, edges, bollards, signs, barriers, gates, fixtures, and pedestrian infrastructure.
Faded markings, missing markings, obstructed signage, and blocked sightlines.
Vegetation overgrowth, weeds, brush, fallen limbs, debris, and access barriers.
Site asset inventory gaps across mapped outdoor zones.

Support copy
Instead of sending teams out only to inspect, Snowbotix collects infrastructure intelligence while the site is already being maintained.

Revised copy: Infrastructure Management
Infrastructure Management

Section headline
Convert inspection data into GIS-ready infrastructure management records.

Body copy
Inspection is only valuable if the data can be used. Snowbotix converts route-level observations into structured records for maintenance planning, asset management, GIS updates, work-order workflows, and operational reporting.

Using sub-inch accurate mapping where configured, Snowbotix can help teams build high-definition digital maps, identify changing asset conditions, create issue records, and prioritize field work based on real site evidence rather than manual walk-throughs or complaint-driven maintenance.

Infrastructure management outputs can include

Sub-inch accurate 3D maps and route-level infrastructure records.
GIS-ready map layers for sidewalks, crossings, ramps, markings, signs, curbs, and outdoor assets.
ArcGIS / GeoJSON exports with API options where configured.
Geotagged and timestamped photos or video.
Automated maintenance tickets.
Condition scoring and flagged photographic evidence.
Asset inventory updates.
Route-by-route inspection history.
Exception records for blocked, damaged, or inaccessible areas.
Maintenance prioritization inputs for facilities, public works, and infrastructure teams.

Support copy
Snowbotix helps teams move from periodic inspections and complaint-driven maintenance to recurring, mapped infrastructure visibility.
